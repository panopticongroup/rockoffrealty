About Us

1% Full-Service Listings!

Without a doubt, the Internet has completely changed how homes are bought and sold. At Rockoff Realty, we keep things efficient. Unlike traditional brokers, we don’t carry the unnecessary and high costs of large office spaces, franchise fees, or big ad budgets. Typical brokers pay for overhead like office rentals, branding fees, and staff—costs that get passed on to clients.

Instead, we operate lean with smart, local marketing, providing the same MLS access, skilled negotiation, and dedicated customer service you expect—at just 1% commission. That’s superior full-service without the extra costs, so you keep more of your home’s value.

So why pay more to sell your home!?

With 500+ homes sold all across Connecticut thus far, our 1% listing fee has saved our clients over $3.0 million since we introduced our pricing model in September 2017!
